17. Sub pedibus solem videt.] Adapted from Virgil, Ecl., V. 57, as to the vision enjoyed
by the glorified shepherd Daphnis. A similar strain of consolation occurs in No. XLIV.

21. Caeli punctum.] Already in Johnston‘s age, through Tycho Brahe, the revelations
of astronomy have made the cultured mind familiar with the littleness of our globe among
the heavenly orbs.
