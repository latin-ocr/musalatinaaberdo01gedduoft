11. Gargaphiae.] The fountain at Plataea in Boeotia. Probably loosely for Boeotiam.

14. Punxit apis.] The pretty story in the Anacreontics of Cupid's being stung by a bee.
17. Facta est.] A particular and single occasion, but veheretur, any occasion.
17. Fabula caelo.] The story of 8th Odyssey, when Venus was entrapped by Vulcan.
