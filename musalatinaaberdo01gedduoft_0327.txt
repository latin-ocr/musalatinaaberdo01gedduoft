86. Cereri.] Human sacrifices to the cognate goddess, Earth or Tellus, are probably
alluded to.

87. Iovis thalamos] Allusion to the audacities of Tantalus, Ixion, and the like.

87. Isthmi] The rapine at the Isthmus of Corinth by Sciron the robber.

92. Rana] The Battle of the Frogs and Mice, attributed to Homer.

93. Achilles.] Thersites was said, in Posthorneric poetry‚ to have been felled by a blow
from the fist of Achilles.

94. Quique—bovem] Phalaris and the brazen bull.
