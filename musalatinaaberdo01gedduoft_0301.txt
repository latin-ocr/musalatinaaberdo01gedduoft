 

 

TO CAELIA. 259

Linquere, maturam carpere disce fugam.
Ah nimis hic atrox et ineluctabile virus
Sentiet, has si quae femina tangat aquas.
Icaris huc veniat, Rhodope vel Thais abibit,
Mille modis reducem fallere docta virum.
Ipsa Diana sinus hic solvet more Diones,
Et Gaditanis ludet, ut illa, modis. 90

90. Gaditanis] The lengthening of the ‘⁴ i,” under metrical needs, as inl. 48, is notable.
